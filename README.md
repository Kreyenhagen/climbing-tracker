This is a climbing exercise progression tracking app that I built using Claude Code. 
The goal was to make something free, open, lightweight, and simple. No accounts, nothing fancy, just you and your progress.
You can set benchmarks, make circuits, custom exercises, and custom workouts.

The app saves your personal data in the browser, so as long as you don't clear your cookies, you'll be good. 
If you do need to clear your cookies, you can export and import your data at the bottom of the Progress page.

To set up the tracker as an app on your iPhone -
1) In Safari, go to kreyenhagen.github.io/climbing-tracker
2) Click the 3 dots in the bottom right
3) Click "Share"
4) Click "View More"
5) Click "Add to Home Screen"
And that's it!
